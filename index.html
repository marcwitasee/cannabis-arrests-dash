How to Use This Dashboard

Welcome to the dashboard! This dashboard shows arrest data for cannabis offenses at the county-level in Colorado from 2012 to 2017. Click on the checkboxes above the map to filter the data by year. Click on a county on the map to filter the data by county. Click the button below to clear the county filter and reset the data for all counties.
